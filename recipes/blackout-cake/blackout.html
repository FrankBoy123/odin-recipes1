Blackout Cake


Description

Blackout cake is a moist and tender cake with deep cocoa flavor. Layers of chocolate cake are filled with light and airy chocolate pudding, then all of this goodness is enrobed in a rich chocolate ganache.

This is a dream dessert: chocolate on chocolate on chocolate.


Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup whole buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup canola oil
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup hot strong brewed coffee
